Research

My work spans supernovae and their progenitor systems, gravitational wave electromagnetic counterparts, and fast radio bursts. I am particularly interested in multi-wavelength follow-up of transients—from radio to X-ray—to constrain explosion physics and progenitor scenarios.

  • Supernovae and progenitor stars
  • Gravitational wave astronomy
  • Fast radio bursts
  • Time-domain surveys (e.g., Young Supernova Experiment)
  • Follow-up imaging and spectroscopy of transients
